# Hello world! 👋
I'm Alex. Full Stack Web Developer based in Barcelona, mostly frontend-focused but very comfortable on the backend too.

- 🔭 Currently working at a tech company building production-grade apps and expanding my stack every day.
- 🌱 Outside of work I co-run a small creative studio building websites for artists and creative teams.

## Bio
Full-stack web developer with 4 years of experience, mostly frontend-focused but very comfortable on the backend too! I work mainly with React, TypeScript and JavaScript, and have built backend services with Node.js, Express and NestJS (love it), dealt with relational and non-relational databases, AWS architectures, SQS queues, Docker environments and CI/CD pipelines with Jenkins.

Lately I've been expanding my stack with Next.js, Astro, knowledge of microservices and architectural patterns, CMS platforms like Strapi and Sanity, and end-to-end testing with Playwright. Outside of work I run a small creative studio with a couple of friends where we build websites for artists and creative teams, something to keep me in touch with my "artsy" side and help non-technical profiles make their own projects.

I care a lot about understanding what I'm building. I think that's the only way to learn!
